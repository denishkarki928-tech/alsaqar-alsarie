---

## ✂️ CapCut build steps (≈20 min)
1. **New project → 9:16** aspect ratio.
2. Drop clips onto the timeline in the order above; trim each to its target length (see table).
3. **Auto Captions:** record your VO first (Record button), then CapCut → *Captions → Auto captions* to generate synced subtitles. Restyle: bold white font, thick outline, bottom-center.
4. Add the **big on-screen text** from the table as separate Text layers (pick a bold font — "Aa → search 'Poppins' or 'Montserrat'"). Animate: *In = Rise / Fade*.
5. Add the **logo** as an overlay (block #7): *Overlay → Add overlay → assets/logo.png*, scale small, top or center.
6. Add music + the swoosh SFX; duck music volume under VO.
7. Optional: *Adjust →* nudge **Brightness/Saturation UP** on the payoff clips (#4–5) to sell the "sparkling" look, and slightly DOWN on the hook (#1–2).
8. **Export → 1080×1920, 30fps.** Done.
